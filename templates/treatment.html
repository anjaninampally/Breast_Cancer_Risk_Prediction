Surgery

Includes lumpectomy or mastectomy. Used in early stages or combined with other treatments.

Radiation Therapy

Uses high-energy rays to kill cancer cells. Typically used after surgery.

Chemotherapy

Drugs that travel through the bloodstream to kill cancer cells. Recommended when risk of spread is high.

Hormonal Therapy

Used for hormone-receptor-positive cancers to block hormones needed for cancer growth.

Targeted Therapy

Targets specific proteins (e.g. HER2) on cancer cells. Less harmful to normal cells.

Support & Lifestyle

Support groups, diet, yoga, and counseling help with recovery and well-being.

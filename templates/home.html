About Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is one of the most common cancers among women. Early detection and prevention can save lives. This tool allows you to assess your risk and learn about preventive actions.

Prevention & Awareness

  • Perform regular self-examinations
  • Get routine mammograms
  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle
  • Limit alcohol and quit smoking
  • Exercise regularly

How Our Predictor Works

Our system uses machine learning (SVM model) to predict your breast cancer risk based on various features. All you need to do is input the required values on the prediction page.
